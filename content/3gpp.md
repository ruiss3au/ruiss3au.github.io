3GPP is where the main actors of the wireless industry meet to build the next generation of mobile communication systems. Now a lot of work is ongoing to build 6G and I've been tracking what happens in 3GPP RAN1 and RAN4 meetings. Below you can find the meetings that we've covered and a schedule of upcoming meetings.
